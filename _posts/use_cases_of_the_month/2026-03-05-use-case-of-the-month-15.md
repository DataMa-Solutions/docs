A new use case showing how Datama help transforming CRM performance analysis at La Centrale - the #1 car marketplace in France - has been published on our blog.

 In this case study, you’ll learn:
* How the team structured their data to bring together CRM, web analytics tools, and transactional data in a single environment
* How they defined a market equation to size potential impacts instead of managing isolated data silos
* How they leveraged Datama in Tableau to provide business teams with a precise, self-service tool to better understand performance and optimize campaigns

Feedback from La Centrale is promising:“CRM teams gain greater autonomy and precision when optimizing every step of their campaigns.”
