In Datama Waterfall, you can refine your analysis in just a few clicks using the segment filter.

* Hover over the segment you want to focus on
* Right-click and select Keep only or Exclude
* The Waterfall chart is instantly recalculated based on your selected scope

A quick way to deep-dive into what really drives your performance 🚀
