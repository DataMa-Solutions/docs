In Explore charts, Waterfalls or decision trees, do you sometimes struggle to find the most intuitive view for your audience, or are you limited by space on your dashboards?

👉 Try the "**Reverse Axis**" feature.

With a single click, it lets you switch from a horizontal view to a vertical one… and vice versa 😉
