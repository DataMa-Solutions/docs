To dig deeper 🔍 into the cause of a change, the **Split Mix Effect and Performance Effect** help you break down the drivers behind your results:

📊 **Mix Effect** highlights the impact of changes in distribution.

📈 **Performance Effect** isolates the actual change in performance.

Both effects are available directly in:

  * 🌊 Waterfall visualizations (by expanding a step when Mix effects are significant)
  * 🧭 Moves visualizations (for a vector view of the variation)

Note: In the settings, there is a “Split Mix & Perf” option where you can choose between “auto,” “always,” or “never” for the display
