With Datama, you can define and update your market equation at different times, depending on your needs.

🛠️ When creating your use case: define your initial equation in Datama Prep whilst configuring your dataset.

🔁 During analysis: adjust it at any time directly in the ‘Settings’ of each solution (Compare, Detect, Assess…) to refine the steps by:
  * adding/removing steps
  * renaming steps
  * defining units
  * and adding ‘Focus’ or ‘Excluded’ dimensions

PS: a handy shortcut using CTRL + F and searching for ‘market equation’ gives you direct access to the market equation menu 😉
