Need to check if you're using the latest **Datama dataViz** version? 👀

Simply open the your extension, click ⚙️ **Settings**, and you'll find your current version displayed in the **bottom-left corner**.

In your [Datama account](app.datama.io), under the ‘My Account’ section and then on the ‘Extensions’ page, select the platform on the right and click on the drop-down menu for the extension you’re interested in (‘latest’ by default) to view all the available versions.

🔄 **How to update?**

📊 **Tableau**: Using the private .trex? Go to the **Extension** page and wait for the  **Update my bucket**.

📈 **Power BI**:
  * Private version → Download the latest package from the **Extension** page.
  * Public version → Updates automatically through Microsoft.

📦 **Qlik**: Upload the latest ZIP downloaded from the **Extension** page.

🌐 **Data Studio**: Wait for the **Update my bucket** on the **Extension** page (you may need to wait for cache refresh ⏳).

📑 **Excel**: Nothing to do — you're always on the latest version. ✅

🟢 **Google Sheets**: Nothing to do — updates follow the latest version published by Google. ✅

🤖 **AI Skill**: Visualization is always up to date, but don't forget to download and update the **instruction ZIP**. 📥

🚀 Keeping your extension up to date means benefiting from the latest features, fixes, and improvements.
