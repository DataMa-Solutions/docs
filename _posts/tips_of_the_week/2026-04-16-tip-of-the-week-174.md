In the Datama menu bar, you have two options:

  * ▶️ 'Compute' allows you to recalculate your charts instantly, without reloading the source data
  * 🔄️ 'Refresh' restarts everything: it clears the cache, reloads the data and completely recalculates the visualisations.

⏱️ Note: if you have an automatic schedule, the cache (data + charts) is retained until the next automatic refresh; otherwise, it expires after 1 hour.

Example: if your workbook is scheduled for every Monday, and you log in on Wednesday, you will have access to the data loaded last Monday. To get new, updated data, you need to run a 'Refresh' ♻️
