A big shift for **anyone who already works with an AI assistant** 🤓

You can now get a **real Datama visualization — waterfall, breakdown, drill-down — delivered straight in the conversation** 🎉

Ask a business question in plain language. When it's relevant, your assistant doesn't just answer in text: it **renders Datama for you**, interactively, in the chat.

* "Why did revenue drop between Q1 and Q2?"
* "Break down conversion by device and channel."
* "What drove the change in margin on mobile vs desktop?"

No switching tools. No rebuilding a chart by hand. **The analysis shows up where you're already thinking.**

➡️ Why teams love it:

* **Stay in the flow:** explore a KPI variation without leaving Claude, Cursor, ChatGPT, or your usual AI workspace.
* **Instant insight:** a full interactive visualization — not a static screenshot — ready to open, share, or dig into.
* **Your data stays yours:** everything runs in the browser; nothing is uploaded to Datama servers for rendering.

Install the **Datama Skill** once from your account, then just ask. The assistant knows when Datama is the right answer.
