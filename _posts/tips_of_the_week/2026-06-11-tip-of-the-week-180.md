A properly configured export ensures that the right information reaches the right people at the right time.

✨ That’s why in the Datama web app, you can schedule your reports to be sent automatically:
  * Via email, Slack or Teams 📩
  * At the frequency of your choice (daily, weekly, monthly... on the days of your choice) ⏰

💡 Be sure to test your export (using the “preview” and/or a test send) before activating it to verify the output and recipients.
