In Datama Light extensions, you can tailor the comments generated by Datama to perfectly match your tone and business needs using the “Edit sentence” feature (accessible via the “Edit template” pop-up window). This allows you to fully customize the wording of the analyses without altering the underlying analysis. ✍️

  🧩 Tailor the wording: modify sentences to match your internal vocabulary or reporting style

  🎯 Highlight what matters: emphasize specific dimensions (e.g., Channel, Region…) in your narrative

  🔁 Use dynamic variables: leverage placeholders such as {step.name}, {step.variation}, or {list} to keep your insights automated while still being personalized

  🎨 Control the formatting: add structure and clarity to make your insights easier to read and share
