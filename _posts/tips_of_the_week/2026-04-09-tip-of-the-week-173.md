With the “Overlay” feature in Datama Explore, display your chart as an overlay to get a clear view of the variations in your KPIs based on the dimensions shown.

For example, when comparing:
  * dates, you’ll see periods with significant variations 🗓️
  * other dimensions, you’ll identify the segments that have varied the most in your comparison 📈

➕ New metrics are available for this visualization, including absolute and relative variations of your indicators.

A simple way to highlight variations! 🚀
