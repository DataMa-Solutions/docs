---

* **Datama Explore Light** now includes several axis improvements:
  * You can now sort charts in ascending or descending order for each axis and legend by clicking the icon next to the axis or legend name.
  * You can now swap X and Y axes using the **Reverse axis** shortcut at the top right of the chart (also available in **Detect** and **Assess**).
  * Discontinuities on continuous axes (for example, missing date ranges) are now displayed as a double slash (`//`) on the axis.
