# 1. Introduction

Thank you for choosing Datama Compare for Excel Add-in, developed by Datama (7 rue Pierre Ginier, 75018 Paris). Datama is committed to protecting the confidentiality and security of user data. This Privacy Policy explains how data is handled when using the Datama Compare for Excel Add-in service.

# 2. Excel Add-in Privacy Policy

## 2.0. Scope of This Policy
This policy applies to the Datama Compare for Excel Add-in service and related processing activities. It does not describe the privacy practices of the Datama marketing website.

## 2.1. Information Collection
Datama Compare for Excel does not sell or monetize your workbook data. The add-in only processes the data needed to deliver analysis features inside Excel.
When personal information is involved (for example, account identifiers, business contact details, support exchanges, or billing/admin metadata), Datama processes it only for service delivery, support, security, and legal compliance.

## 2.2. User Consent
By installing and using the add-in, users and administrators accept the data practices described in this policy and in Microsoft 365 add-in governance settings.

## 2.3. Data Storage and International Transfers
Data remains under customer control through Microsoft 365 and customer-managed environments. When processing is required to provide the service, Datama applies appropriate safeguards and limits retention to what is necessary for service operation and support.

## 2.4. Sharing of Information
Datama does not sell, lease, or broker personal data. Data is shared only with subprocessors required to operate the service, under contractual confidentiality and security obligations.

## 2.5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies
The Excel Add-in may use strictly necessary technical storage mechanisms (for example, local/session storage or equivalent) to maintain user settings and session continuity. No advertising trackers are used.

## 2.6. Third-party Access
Third-party access is restricted to authorized service providers strictly required for hosting, monitoring, and support, with least-privilege access controls.

## 2.7. Data Security
Datama follows Microsoft Office Add-in security principles:
- HTTPS/TLS is used for add-in endpoints and network communications.
- The add-in runs in a browser-based runtime sandbox (WebView) isolated from the host Office process.
- Access to workbook features is constrained by Office add-in permissions and manifest declarations.
- Sensitive secrets (such as passwords or long-lived credentials) must not be stored in Office settings objects.

## 2.8. Notification in Case of Data Breach
In the event of a confirmed security incident affecting customer data, Datama will notify impacted customers without undue delay, according to contractual and legal obligations.

## 2.9. Links to Other Sites
This documentation may include links to external resources (for example, Microsoft documentation). Datama is not responsible for third-party privacy practices.

## 2.10. Unsubscribing
Users can stop using the add-in at any time by removing it from Excel or through Microsoft 365 admin controls.

## 2.11. Changes to This Privacy Policy
This policy may be updated to reflect product, legal, or security changes. The latest version is published on Datama documentation pages.

## 2.12. Children
Datama Compare for Excel is intended for professional/business use and is not designed for children under 13.

For any questions or feedback regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at contact@Datama.fr.
