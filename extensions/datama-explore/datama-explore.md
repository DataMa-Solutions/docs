> **Datama Explore (Dimension Helper) allows to breakdown any pair of metrics on any pair of dimensions for a detailed granular analysis about yours different KPIs**

<br>

This module, which has full app version (screenshot above) and extension version (screenshot below), allows you to slice and dice your data across two dimensions and two metrics, giving you the flexibility to choose the dimension pairs and metric pairs you want to analyze, along with the visualization type that best fits your needs (with Datama Explore entension).
